Fried Peanut Butter and Jelly

So what is Fried Peanut Butter and Jelly???

This fried peanut butter and jelly sandwich is simple, yet so delicious. It’s savory, sweet, and the crisp bit of the fried bread makes it a treat that you’ve got to try!

So to put these fried peanut butter and jelly together, its not as hard as you’d think

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 1/4 cup of peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup of jelly
  • 2 cups of cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat a cast iron skillet or fryer to 375 F
  2. Flatten your bread with a rolling pin, spread half the slice of bread with peanut butter, the other half with your favorite jelly.
  3. Fold in half and crimp the edges. Optionally you can use a tool like an empanada press or an uncrustable maker.
  4. Fry for about 1-2 minutes until golden brown flipping halfway if not fully submerged.
  5. Let it cool on a wire rack for acouple minutes; sprinkle with powdered sugar and enjoy!
