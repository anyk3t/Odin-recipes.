Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter sauce.

Butter Chicken is one of the most popular Indian curries favored by people around the world.

This mildly spiced creamy chicken curry is well suited for any occasions and served in all kinds of eateries.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion
  • ½ inch sized ginger
  • 10 oz can tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp kashmiri powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 1 cup milk cream
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • oil
  • salt
  • cilantro leaves

Instructions

  1. Take fresh ingredients in a huge utensil
  2. Boil the mixture and blend
  3. Prepare tadka for gravy
  4. Cook gravy properly
  5. Marinade chicken
  6. Cook chicken in a tandoor
  7. Take out cooked chicken
  8. Mix gravy and chicken together
